## Business Problem
The coffee business generates a substantial amount of transactional sales data; however, this data is not being effectively utilized to support strategic decision-making. This lack of insight can hinder the business’s ability to optimize its product offerings, improve operational efficiency, and maximize revenue potential. Therefore, the core challenge is to systematically analyze the available sales data and transform it into actionable insights that enable informed, data-driven business decisions.

---

## How I Solved the Problem
To address this problem, I used Excel and SQL to clean, organize, and analyze the coffee sales dataset.

First, I prepared the data in Excel by handling missing values, removing duplicates, and ensuring consistency across all fields.

Next, I applied SQL queries to explore and analyze the data at scale. Using SQL operations such as aggregations, grouping, filtering, and sorting, I identified key insights including total revenue, top-selling products, high-value customers, and sales trends over time.

This structured approach allowed me to convert raw transactional data into meaningful insights, helping the business better understand its performance and make informed, data-driven decisions.
